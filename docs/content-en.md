## 2. About Us
JELIRO Metals Trading FZCO is an international commodities trading company headquartered in Dubai (Dubai Silicon Oasis). We specialize in the sourcing, trading and export of tin ore (cassiterite), refined tin and non-ferrous metals. Backed by upstream mining operations, we connect dependable mineral supply with smelters and industrial buyers across Asia, the Americas and beyond.
